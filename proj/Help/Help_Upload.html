Upload

Overview

This is a module only accessable by radiologists and the system administrator. It is used to upload new radiology records to the database and to upload images to an existing radiology record.

How to Upload a New Record

There is a form to fill out all the necessary information required to generate a new record. There are 9 fields to fill out: Record ID, Patient ID, Radiologist ID, Doctor ID, Test Type, Prescribing Date, Test Date, Diagnosis, Description. The absolutely required fields for a new form re Record ID, Patient ID, Radiologist ID, and Doctor ID.

Once sufficient fields have been filled out, press the Upload Record button to Upload the new record.

How to Upload Images

To Upload an image to an existing radiology record, you must fill in two fields in the Upload Image form; Record ID, and the image you are uploading. First select a record id from the drop down menu. Second, select an image from your local filesystem of the form jpg, jpeg, x-png, png, and smaller than 1600x1600. Once both fields are filled out press the upload image button to upload the image to the specified record.
